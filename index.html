Shawn Scamell

Did you know the fastest way to grow as a developer isn’t just working on client projects — it’s building for the joy of creating?

I’m Shawn Scamell, a Software Engineer with a 2:1 BSc in Software Engineering from The University of Portsmouth, based in Essex, UK. I specialise in full-stack web development — designing clean, responsive interfaces and building robust, scalable back-end systems that bring ideas to life from the first line of code to the finished product.

Some of my projects are focused on solving real problems, creating smarter applications, and experimenting with new frameworks. Others? They’re just for fun — the kind of builds that start with curiosity and end up teaching me something unexpected. Either way, every project sharpens my skills and pushes me further as a developer.

Alongside web development, I work with data analytics, SQL, and Python, giving my applications an extra layer of intelligence and capability. Whether practical or playful, my projects are always built with care, clarity, and purpose.
